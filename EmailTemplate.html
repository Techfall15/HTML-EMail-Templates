My travel tip of the month is: ROOM UPGRADES!

Even though you always get the best experiences at the best prices available when working with our travel guru's, it never hurts to ask for an upgrade when checking in. In which case, you are right where you were to begin with. So be kind, ask nicely, and enjoy the rest of your trip!
